The TripFlightHelper browser add-on is brought to you by yxuk@ctrip.com. This notice describes our privacy policy. This Policy and terms of use helps you to understand what information we collect from you, when you use the TripFlightHelper Add-on and how we use that information. It also includes some terms relating to the permitted uses of the Add-on. By downloading the Add-on you confirm that you have read and agree to the terms of this Policy.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

The Add-on does not collect any personally identifiable information from you through your downloading and use of the Add-on.

When you use your browser to search for flight tickets on certain travel sites, the Add-on enables your browser automatically to check if trip.com is offering the same airline tickets at a lower price. If that airline ticket is available on trip.com at a lower price the Add-on will notify you of the lower priced airline ticket and provides you with a link to trip.com offering the lower priced airline ticket.

TripFlightHelper do, however, collect the following information via the Add-on:

  • the URL of the site that you get the airline ticket details on.
  • the characteristics of the airline tickets that you search for.

COOKIES

The Add-on uses cookies to collect the information referred to above and to ensure you get the most benefit out of the Add-on. Cookies are small amounts of information that are stored on your computer that allow us to monitor website traffic.

HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

TripFlightHelper will only use the information that is referred to above for the following purposes:

  • to enable the Add-on to retrieve information for you about lower priced products from Trip.com.
  • to enable us to optimise the performance of the Add-on.
